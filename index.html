Our History

Built in 1892, Papaya Palm Resorts stands as a testament to Victorian opulence and charm. Originally conceived as a retreat for affluent travelers, its ornate façade and luxurious interiors soon became a symbol of prestige. Throughout its storied history, the hotel has hosted illustrious guests, from dignitaries to Hollywood celebrities, each leaving their mark on its rich tapestry of memories.
