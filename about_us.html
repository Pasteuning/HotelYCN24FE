Our History

Throughout its storied history, the hotel has hosted illustrious guests, from dignitaries to Hollywood celebrities, each leaving their mark on its rich tapestry of memories. Surviving through economic upheavals and wars, it has remained a beacon of hospitality, evolving with the times while preserving its timeless elegance. Today, Papaya Palm Resorts continues to enchant visitors with its blend of historic grandeur and modern comforts, offering a glimpse into a bygone era with every stay. Its walls whisper tales of romance, adventure, and enduring legacy, making it a cherished landmark in the heart of the city.
